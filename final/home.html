Greenland’s coolest jobs

Their traditional way of life is under threat – meet the entrepreneurial Greenlanders adapting their day jobs.

With its blue glaciers and stark, snowy, landscapes that stretch beyond the horizon, Greenland is the one of the few places on earth where Instagram filters are utterly redundant.

Stunningly beautiful yet also one of the harshest climates imaginable,Greenlanders need to adapt to work and survive here. It’s both the world’s biggest island (excluding Australia) and the 12th biggest country. Yet the population of 56,000 is similar to a small city.
